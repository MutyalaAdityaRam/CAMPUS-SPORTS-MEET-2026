About the Meet

The Campus Sports Meet 2026 brings students from all departments together for friendly competition, teamwork, and active campus spirit. Students can check event details, submit participation entries, and share feedback after the events.

Important Announcements

  • Spot registration closes 30 minutes before each open event.
  • Team captains must provide complete member count during registration.
  • Students must carry college ID cards to the venue.
  • Feedback forms will be reviewed by the Sports Committee.
